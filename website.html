Welcome to Text4Weather
What is Text4Weather?


Text4Weather enables people with "dumb" phones or people without a data plan to recieve weather information to their phones without the use of data such as Wi-Fi, 3G, and 4G. It relies solely upon SMS to function! Text4Weather is achieved by utilizing various technologies such as the Twilio API, OpenWeatherMap API, and various Python libraries.
